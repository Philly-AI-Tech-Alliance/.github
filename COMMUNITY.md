## Ways to Contribute

### For Everyone
- Join discussions and share your ideas
- Answer questions in Q&A
- Welcome and help new members
- Share your projects in Show & Tell
- Attend events and workshops

### For Developers
- Browse [good first issues](https://github.com/search?q=org%3APhilly-AI-Tech-Alliance+label%3Agood-first-issue+state%3Aopen&type=issues)
- Review pull requests
- Fix bugs and add features
- Improve documentation and tests

### For Researchers & Educators
- Share research findings and papers
- Create tutorials and educational content
- Lead workshops or study groups
- Propose research collaborations

### For Community Builders
- Organize events and meetups
- Create content (blogs, videos, podcasts)
- Build partnerships with other organizations
- Help with outreach and growth

## Getting Help

### Where to Ask Questions
- **General questions**: GitHub Discussions Q&A category
- **Technical issues**: Comment on relevant issues or PRs
- **Private matters**: Email core-team@philly-ai-tech-alliance.org (when available)
- **Real-time help**: Slack #help channel (when available)

### Response Times
- GitHub Discussions: Usually within 24-48 hours
- Pull Request reviews: Within 3-5 business days
- Urgent issues: Tag with `priority: high` and explain why

## Recognition & Growth

We value and recognize contributions at every level:

- **Contributors**: Anyone who makes a contribution (code, docs, discussions, events)
- **Regular Contributors**: Consistent, meaningful contributions over time
- **Maintainers**: Trusted community members with repository responsibilities
- **Core Team**: Organization leaders and decision-makers

Learn more about progression: [Contributor Journey](docs/CONTRIBUTOR-JOURNEY.md)
