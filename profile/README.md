

**Building Philadelphia's tech community, one commit at a time.**

Welcome to the Coffee Code Philly Accelerator GitHub organization! We're a vibrant community of developers, designers, entrepreneurs, and tech enthusiasts working together to build innovative projects, learn new skills, and strengthen Philadelphia's tech ecosystem.

---

## 🚀 What We Do

- **🏗️ Build Together**: Collaborative projects that solve real problems in our community
- **📚 Learn Together**: Workshops, code sessions, and peer-to-peer learning
- **🤝 Connect**: Regular meetups, hackathons, and networking events
- **🌱 Grow**: Mentorship opportunities and skill development
- **🎯 Launch**: Support for entrepreneurs and project founders

## 🎯 Getting Involved

### 🆕 New to the Community?

1. **Join an Event**: Visit [phillycodes.rsvp](https://phillycodes.rsvp) and RSVP for an upcoming session
2. **Connect Online**: Join our Discord/Slack (links on phillycodes.rsvp)
3. **Browse Issues**: Look for issues labeled [`good-first-issue`](https://github.com/search?q=org%3ACoffee-Code-Philly-Accelerator+label%3Agood-first-issue+state%3Aopen&type=issues)
4. **Read Our Guides**: Check out [CONTRIBUTING.md](https://github.com/Coffee-Code-Philly-Accelerator/.github/blob/main/.github/CONTRIBUTING.md)

### 💻 For Developers

- **Find a Project**: Explore our repositories and find one that interests you
- **Pick an Issue**: Look for [`help-wanted`](https://github.com/search?q=org%3ACoffee-Code-Philly-Accelerator+label%3Ahelp-wanted+state%3Aopen&type=issues) labels
- **Submit a PR**: Follow our [contribution guidelines](https://github.com/Coffee-Code-Philly-Accelerator/.github/blob/main/.github/CONTRIBUTING.md)
- **Get Reviewed**: Maintainers will review and provide feedback

### 🎨 For Designers

- **UI/UX**: Help improve user experience across our projects
- **Marketing**: Create visual assets for events and social media
- **Branding**: Contribute to our visual identity

### 📝 For Writers

- **Documentation**: Improve READMEs, guides, and tutorials
- **Blogging**: Write about your experience building with us
- **Translation**: Help make our projects accessible to more people

### 🎤 For Community Leaders

- **Organize Events**: Help plan and run Coffee & Code sessions
- **Mentorship**: Guide new contributors and junior developers
- **Outreach**: Spread the word and grow our community

## 🤝 How We Work

### Our Values

**🌍 Inclusive**: Everyone is welcome, regardless of background or experience level
